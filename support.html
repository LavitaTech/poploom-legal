Common Questions

Game Center / Leaderboards: Make sure you’re signed into Game Center in your device settings. If leaderboards are not loading, reopen the app and check your network connection.

Local Scores: Your local leaderboard is stored on your device. Deleting the app may remove local gameplay history.

Gameplay Issues: If the game feels unresponsive or you notice performance issues, restarting the app can help.

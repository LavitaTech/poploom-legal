Poploom – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2, 2026

Poploom (“the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

Poploom does not collect personal information directly from users. The App may use Apple services such as Game Center to support features like global leaderboards. Apple manages Game Center data under its own policies.

Game Center & Leaderboards: If you use global leaderboard features, the App submits gameplay scores and best streaks to Apple Game Center so they can be ranked. Your Game Center nickname and scores may be visible to other players through Game Center rankings.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

Poploom does not directly collect:

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or contacts
  • Precise location data
  • Photos, camera, microphone, or files
  • In-app chat or messaging content
  • Payment information

3. Local Gameplay Data

Poploom may save local gameplay data on your device, such as your local high score, best streak, and recent local runs. This local data is used to display your personal leaderboard and improve the gameplay experience.

4. Analytics

Poploom may use privacy-friendly diagnostics or Apple-provided analytics to understand stability and improve gameplay performance. Poploom does not sell user data.

5. Advertising and Tracking

Poploom does not currently include third-party advertising. If advertising is added in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated, and the App will request permission where required by Apple policies and applicable law.

6. Children’s Privacy

Poploom does not knowingly collect personal data from children. Any data associated with Game Center is handled by Apple.

7. Third-Party Services

Poploom relies on Apple Game Center for global leaderboards. Use of Apple services is governed by Apple’s applicable terms and privacy policies.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

9. Contact
